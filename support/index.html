Support

We read every message. Email us and we will get back to you as soon as we can.

All support requests go to one inbox. Please use the email subject format shown for your category so we can route and prioritize your message correctly.

Email support

Send mail to prakshatechnologies@gmail.com. Tap a category below to open your mail app with the subject line filled in, or copy the subject manually.

Write to prakshatechnologies@gmail.com

Before you email

  • One topic per email. If you have two unrelated issues, send two messages with the matching subjects.
  • Device and app version. Include your phone model, Android version, and the app version shown in Settings (if available).
  • Steps to reproduce for bugs: what you tapped, what you expected, and what happened instead.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings help for visual glitches, crashes after a specific screen, or feed problems.
  • Do not send passwords or payment card numbers. We will never ask for them by email.
