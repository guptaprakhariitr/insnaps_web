Privacy Policy

InSnaps : Geopolitics & War News — Last updated: April 16, 2026

InSnaps ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is a geopolitics and world news aggregator developed by Renu Gupta. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

Summary: We collect minimal data. We use Google Sign-In for authentication, Firebase for analytics and data sync, and Google AdMob for advertising. We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information you provide via Google Sign-In

When you sign in with your Google account, we receive:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo URL
  • Google user ID

Sign-in is optional. You can use the App without signing in, but some features (premium subscriptions, cross-device sync of preferences) require authentication.

b) Information collected automatically

Data Purpose Stored Where
App usage analytics (screens viewed, features used, session duration) Improve the app experience Firebase Analytics (Google)
Device type, OS version, app version Crash diagnosis, compatibility Firebase Analytics
Feed scroll count, articles read, days active, shares count App engagement stats (shown to you in Settings) Device locally + Firebase Firestore (if signed in)
Notification preferences, app language, theme preference Personalize your experience Device locally + Firebase Firestore (if signed in)

c) Purchase information

If you subscribe to InSnaps Pro, purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We receive:

  • Product ID (which plan you purchased)
  • Transaction ID
  • Transaction date
  • Subscription status (active/expired)

We do not receive or store your payment card details, billing address, or bank information. All payment processing is handled by Apple or Google.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To identify you and sync your preferences and premium status across devices.
  • Premium subscriptions: To verify your subscription status and manage your Pro features.
  • Personalization: To save your language preference, theme, notification settings, and country selection.
  • Analytics: To understand how the App is used and improve it. Analytics data is aggregated and not used to identify individual users.
  • Advertising: To display ads via Google AdMob. Pro subscribers see no ads.
  • Notifications: To send you local news notifications at your preferred times (scheduled on your device, not from our servers).

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud): Stores user profiles, premium status, and preferences for signed-in users. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Local device storage (SharedPreferences, SQLite): Stores cached articles, preferences, and engagement stats. This data stays on your device.
  • We do not operate our own backend servers for user data. All cloud storage is through Google Firebase.

5. News Content

InSnaps aggregates publicly available news from RSS feeds and public APIs. We do not create, edit, or endorse the news content displayed in the App. Articles are sourced from publicly available news publishers and link to the original sources.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your data may be shared only in these circumstances:

  • With Google: As part of Firebase and AdMob services described above.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Use without signing in: You can browse the feed, read articles, play games, and use most features without creating an account.
  • Disable notifications: Turn off notifications in the App settings or your device settings at any time.
  • Opt out of personalized ads: You can reset your Advertising ID or limit ad tracking in your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking; Android: Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Delete your account: Contact us at the email below and we will delete your data from Firebase Firestore within 30 days.
  • Sign out: You can sign out at any time from Settings, which stops data syncing to the cloud.
  • Data export: Contact us to request a copy of your data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

9. Data Retention

  • Account data (profile, preferences, premium status): Retained as long as your account exists. Deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.
  • Analytics data: Retained by Firebase Analytics for up to 14 months, then automatically deleted.
  • Local data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data.

10. International Users

Your data is processed and stored on Google Firebase servers, which may be located outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these servers. Google Firebase complies with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
