Privacy Policy

InSnaps: Celebrity Monitor - Last updated: May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how InSnaps: Celebrity Monitor ("App", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information. The App is operated by Praksha Technologies.

Contact: prakshatechnologies@gmail.com

1) Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and account ID when you sign in with Google or Apple via Firebase Authentication.
  • Device and app information: device model, OS version, app version, language, country, and diagnostics.
  • Analytics information: events such as screens viewed, taps, session duration, and feature usage.
  • Notification information: Firebase Cloud Messaging push token and notification interaction status.
  • Advertising identifiers: IDFA/GAID or equivalent identifiers used by Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), subject to your device settings and consent choices.
  • User content and preferences: saved articles, followed celebrity preferences, and app settings (stored locally, and in cloud when sync is enabled).
  • Deep-link metadata: link token or path parameters needed to open in-app content using deep links/universal links.

2) How We Collect Data

  • Directly from you: when you create/sign in to an account, set preferences, or contact support.
  • Automatically: through app usage, device signals, and integrated SDKs.
  • From third parties: sign-in providers (Google/Apple), analytics/ad partners, and app platforms.

3) Why We Use Data

  • Account login and authentication.
  • Delivering notifications and alerts.
  • App performance monitoring, analytics, and product improvements.
  • Personalization, including saved articles and followed celebrity preferences.
  • Ad delivery and ad measurement (where applicable).
  • Fraud prevention, abuse detection, and app security.
  • Support requests and troubleshooting.

4) Third-Party Services We Use

  • Firebase Authentication (Google/Apple sign-in): account login.
  • Firebase Firestore: user profile and synced data storage (if enabled).
  • Firebase Remote Config: feature/config delivery.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: push notifications and push token management.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): ad serving, ad identifiers, and ad measurement.
  • Apple and Google app platforms: app distribution and platform services.

These providers have their own privacy terms: Firebase, Google, Google Ads, Apple.

5) Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data only:

  • With service providers/processors listed above to operate the app.
  • When required by law, legal process, or government request.
  • To protect rights, safety, users, or platform integrity.
  • During a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, with notice where required.

6) Data Retention

  • Account and synced data: retained while your account is active, then deleted on request within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days, except legal/backup constraints).
  • Analytics data: retained per provider settings and legal requirements.
  • Local app data: remains on your device until you clear data or uninstall the app.
  • Backups: may persist for a limited period before automatic deletion.

7) Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data.

  • Request access, correction, or deletion by contacting us at prakshatechnologies@gmail.com.
